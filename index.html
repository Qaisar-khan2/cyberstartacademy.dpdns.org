OUR MISSION

About CyberStart Academy

Building the next generation of ethical cybersecurity professionals.

Who We Are

CyberStart Academy was founded by a team of cybersecurity professionals, ethical hackers, and educators with a shared mission: to make high-quality cybersecurity education accessible to everyone, everywhere — while keeping it completely legal and ethical.

We believe that understanding offensive security techniques is essential for building strong defenses. Our curriculum teaches students to think like attackers so they can defend like experts.

Our Core Values

  • ◆ Ethics First — Every technique is taught within a strict legal framework
  • ◆ Hands-On Learning — 60% lab work, 40% theory
  • ◆ Community Driven — Learn with 48,500+ fellow students
  • ◆ Industry Aligned — Content maps to CEH, OSCP, CompTIA Security+
